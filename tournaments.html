2024 December Open Chess Tournament

December 14, 2024



SLCA's 4th annual Open Chess Tournament was a huge success! Held at the Burlington's Haber Recreational Center, highschoolers from across the Halton and Peel regions came to have fun and bond over their shared love of chess.

The rounds were played using rapid timings, with 3 blitz rounds at the end to excite everyone.

Congratulations to the winners!

2024 Halton Chess Championship

May 30, 2024

2024 HCC was a blast! Chess clubs from all over Halton convened at the Milton Mall, and there was some fierce competition. After 3 hours of chess teams dueling it out, the 2 teams left were Abbey Park High School and, reigning champ, White Oaks Secondary School. After a highly anticipated battle between the 2 teams, APHS claimed victorous, stealing the title from WOSS and achieving their first win in Ontario SLCA!

The only question that remains... will WOSS take back their title? Or will a new competitor emerge?
